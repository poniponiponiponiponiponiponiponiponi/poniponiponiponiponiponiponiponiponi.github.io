About

Greetings! I’m Adrian, a programmer interested in exploit development, high-performance computing, operating systems and emulation, among other stuff. Currently studying computer engineering in Wrocław, Poland.

I play CTF cybersecurity competitions focusing on binary exploitation. Nowadays you will see me playing with a local polish team WROng_flag. In 2025 we got 48th place on CTFTime’s global ranking. In 2023 I was playing with some internet friends as sillysec where we got 70th place globally.

I’m a member of the scientific circle PWr WhiteHats. As a challenge author and co-organizer, I’ve worked on multiple CTF competitions. Most notably BreakTheSyntax CTF 2025 and CyberTrust CTF 2025. Also made some challenges for CyberAdvent WSIZ 2025 and WxMCTF 2024.

Other interests worth listing:

  • Free and open-source software. Linux and Emacs <3
  • Overall just media consumption. Music, otaku culture, video games, etc.
  • Casual interest in psychoanalysis.
